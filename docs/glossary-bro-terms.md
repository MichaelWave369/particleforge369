# Bro Terms Glossary

## Field

A value spread through space. In bro terms: the invisible ocean the particles move through.

## Boson

A particle connected to a field or force-style behavior.

## Event

One simulated collision or collision-like snapshot.

## Channel

A specific way a particle can decay or be detected.

## Histogram

A stack of many events so a hidden pattern can rise above noise.

## Receipt

A structured record of what was claimed, what was simulated, and what boundary applies.
